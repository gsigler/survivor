## Episode 1

Savannah openly revealed she had won Season 49 just two weeks prior, hoping transparency would build trust. On Day 4 she beat Colby in a backwards Jenga/stacking challenge and earned a Block-a-Vote, which she kept completely secret. But her tribemates (especially Rick Devens) found her story about the Journey unconvincing and suspect she's hiding something.
