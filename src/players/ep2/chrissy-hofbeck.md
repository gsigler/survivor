Chrissy was the most vocal early strategist on Kalo, immediately pushing to target Dee as the tribe's first boot due to her winner status. However, the New Era women's alliance (Dee, Tiffany, Kamilla) identified Chrissy as THEIR primary target, remembering how she gunned for younger women in HHH.
