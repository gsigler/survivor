## Episode 1

Christian made fire using his eyeglasses on Day 1, earning instant goodwill. He was the first to identify Cirie as a threat but stayed quiet, instead using Rick Devens to relay info to Cirie that Jenna was targeting her. This effectively framed Jenna as the sole instigator and led to a unanimous 7-1 vote against her. Jenna believed Christian was her ally and later said he "set her up."

## Episode 2

Christian found Cila's Boomerang Idol and gifted it to Aubry Bracco on Vatu as a "token of trust," hoping to build a cross-tribal bond between fellow superfan strategists. He then hatched a scheme with Rick Devens to plant the idol parchment at Tribal Council as a decoy for future use. He experienced gastrointestinal distress early in the episode but recovered to play a central strategic role. The vote landed on Savannah as Cirie's campaign succeeded.

## Episode 3

The episode opened with Christian and Rick celebrating their Episode 2 "heist" -- Christian had faked a fall at Tribal Council ("Tribal Council makes me woozy sometimes") to distract while Rick planted the fake idol parchment behind a rock near the fire pit. They called it the greatest heist in Survivor history. Before the swap, they also agreed to lie that Rick found the Boomerang Idol (not Christian), and told Cirie this cover story too. When the swap hit, Christian had no time to loop Emily in -- he whispered to her at the challenge mat that "Aubry has an idol," which upset Emily for being told last-minute. On new Vatu, he reunited with his David vs. Goliath castmates Angelina and Mike, forming a natural trio -- though he noted both Mike and Angelina had tried to vote him out multiple times in Season 37. His challenge calling was a disaster -- his tribe was openly hostile as he failed to navigate Q and Ozzy through the blindfolded course, costing Vatu the immunity win. At Tribal Council, the DvG trio teamed with Emily and Ozzy to vote out Q 5-1. This was Christian's third straight Tribal Council.

## Episode 4

Mike made his final move: warning Christian directly that Emily would betray him, just as Gabby Pascuzzi had done in Season 37 -- calling it a "knife to the heart." It was a devastating emotional appeal. But Christian processed it, concluded that Mike was the more sophisticated manipulator and therefore the greater long-term threat, and voted him out. He aligned with Emily and Stephenie to send Mike home 3-2-1. No advantages were played. Christian now sits atop a reshuffled Vatu with his two votes alongside him, and his Boomerang Idol insurance through Aubry still intact.

## Episode 5

Christian faced backlash from Ozzy over the Mike White blindside. Ozzy compared being left out to Cochran's betrayal in South Pacific and demanded Christian hand over his Shot in the Dark as a loyalty test. Privately furious ("the ritual of subservience to the great Ozzy of the island"), Christian gave it over as a calculated move to lull Ozzy into a false sense of security — a plan Emily helped him see as strategically correct. The move worked: Ozzy felt trusted and joined the unified vote against Angelina (4-1). Christian has now been to every tribal council and written a correct name every time. He is the last remaining David vs. Goliath player.
