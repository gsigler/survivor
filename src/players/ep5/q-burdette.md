## Episode 1

Q stepped up for the supply challenge and was sent to Exile with Ozzy. He agreed to trade his vote for camp supplies while Ozzy took the Extra Vote. When he returned to camp, he initially lied about what happened, fabricating a story about a coconut-carrying challenge. After Kyle brought him into the majority alliance, Q came clean about losing his vote. When Colby then openly told the tribe about his own lost vote, Q was alarmed, realizing the majority was severely vote-deficient.

## Episode 2

Q remained part of the Vatu majority as the core four (Q, Colby, Genevieve, Stephenie) recruited Rizo into their alliance. Vatu won immunity again, so Q's lost vote was not tested. He is "course-correcting" his aggressive behavior from Season 46, playing a more measured game.

## Episode 3

The swap landed Q on new Vatu with Ozzy, Emily, Christian, Mike, Angelina, and Stephenie. He tried to build trust with Ozzy by revealing that Vatu had sent him the Boomerang Idol and admitting he had lost his vote. He formed an alliance with Ozzy, Emily, and Stephenie, targeting Angelina. But his plan unraveled: he lied about having an Extra Vote and told Mike to vote for him -- part of a convoluted scheme to force a tie on the revote, where his three allies could then eliminate Angelina. The plan backfired completely when Emily flipped to the DvG trio and convinced Ozzy to follow. Q was voted out 5-1, with only Stephenie voting his way (for Angelina). Before his torch was snuffed, Q echoed his Season 46 move by "welcoming" the votes, but this time it was pure desperation rather than strategy.
