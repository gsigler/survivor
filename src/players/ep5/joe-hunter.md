## Episode 1

Joe was initially receptive to Jenna's pitch to vote out Cirie. He and Savannah had a side conversation about the Ozzy-Cirie pair being too tight, comparing it to getting burned by Kyle and Kamilla in Season 48. Ultimately he took a conservative, keep-the-tribe-strong approach and voted with the majority against Jenna.

## Episode 2

Joe clashed with Rick Devens over whispered conversations between Rick and Savannah, growing suspicious that people were lying to him. He demanded transparency about alliance membership at camp, creating conflict. He was a potential target before Cirie redirected the vote onto Savannah. Joe's confrontational approach put him on shaky ground heading forward.

## Episode 3

The swap was a lifeline for Joe, landing him on new Kalo far from his Cila rivals Rick and Christian. He immediately gravitated toward Coach and Colby, forming an alliance built on "good core values" -- a natural fit for his direct, no-nonsense style. Coach brought in Chrissy as a fourth, giving the group a solid majority on the seven-person tribe. After two episodes of being on the outs at Cila, Joe is now in the best position of his game. Kalo won second-place immunity.

## Episode 4

Kalo won both the immunity challenge and reward -- a private spearfishing trip and a Zac Brown concert. Joe enjoyed the spoils alongside his alliance. No threats, no Tribal Council, just a strong position on a winning tribe. His game has transformed completely since the swap.
