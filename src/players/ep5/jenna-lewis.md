## Episode 1

Jenna came in with a clear target: Cirie Fields. She rallied support and believed she had the numbers, but Christian used Rick to tip Cirie off, and the entire tribe flipped. Jenna was voted out 7-1 in the season's first Tribal Council. She later said Christian "set her up."
