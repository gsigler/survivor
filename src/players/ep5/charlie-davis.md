## Episode 1

Charlie had a quieter premiere but bonded closely with Coach, who gave him a toe ring that hadn't been off his body in 37 years. Mike White also approached him about working together. Pre-game intel suggests Jonathan wants to target Charlie early. Kalo won immunity so no Tribal Council.

## Episode 2

Charlie continued to lay low as Kalo won both the reward and immunity challenges, avoiding Tribal Council for the second straight week. A generational divide is emerging on the tribe, with the younger players (Charlie, Dee, Tiffany, Kamilla) on one side and the older players (Coach, Chrissy) on the other. Charlie's position between the two groups makes him a potential swing vote.

## Episode 3

The swap landed Charlie on new Cila with three other Kalo members (Dee, Kamilla, Jonathan), plus Rizo (Vatu), Cirie, and Rick (Cila). The Kalo four hold the numbers. In a nighttime conversation, Rizo revealed he had voted against his Season 49 bestie Sophie at Final Tribal, which triggered Charlie's Survivor 46 PTSD about being screwed by his close ally Maria at FTC. Rizo even asked Charlie about his relationship with Maria, an unwitting reminder of that painful experience. For Charlie, Rizo became an instant red flag and likely first target if Cila goes to Tribal.

## Episode 4

Cila placed second in the "Arch Nemesis" immunity challenge, narrowly missing out on the Zac Brown concert reward. With no Tribal Council to attend, Charlie turned his focus inward. His distrust of Rizo deepened further, and he began quietly laying the groundwork for what he's calling "Operation Bad Blood" -- a plan to engineer Rizo's exit. He's being deliberate and patient, not showing his hand yet, but the wheels are turning.
