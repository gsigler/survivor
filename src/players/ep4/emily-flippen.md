## Episode 1

Emily was pulled into the Christian-Rick duo as a third member early on. She pitched voting out Ozzy instead of Jenna or Cirie, arguing it would break up the dangerous Ozzy-Cirie pair. Rick shared this preference, but the trio ultimately went with the majority to vote out Jenna. When Rick approached her about working together, she said "I could kiss you!"

## Episode 2

Emily had a quieter second episode. She initially pushed to target Ozzy but went along with Cirie's campaign to vote out Savannah instead. She remains a solid third in the Christian-Rick-Emily trio, which emerged as the swing vote bloc at Cila's second Tribal Council.

## Episode 3

Emily's game exploded in the swap episode. As the tribe swap was announced, Christian rushed to whisper to her at the mat that "Aubry has an idol" -- information she was upset about receiving last-minute, feeling left out of major alliance conversations. On new Vatu, Q approached her to form a foursome with Ozzy and Stephenie, but Emily felt they were "driving the ship and not giving her options, but directions." She went to Angelina and Christian and shared everything -- including that Aubry had the Boomerang Idol, blowing Christian's secret wide open. She admitted she's "so terrible about keeping information to myself." Emily became the tribe's undeniable swing vote. Ultimately, she sided with the DvG trio and convinced Ozzy to join them in voting out Q 5-1. Q later called her gameplay "sloppy" but acknowledged she was always going to side with Christian.

## Episode 4

Emily's read on Mike White proved correct — he tried to use her as the target by invoking Gabby Pascuzzi to Christian, but Christian looped Emily in instead of freezing her out. Emily voted Mike out 3-2-1 with Christian and Stephenie, becoming the third member of an informal new trio. Mike's vote was against her and Ozzy voted for Angelina, meaning only the three of them were coordinated. Emily has been the swing vote at multiple tribals and is emerging as a subtle strategic force.
