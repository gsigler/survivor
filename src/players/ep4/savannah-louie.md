## Episode 1

Savannah openly revealed she had won Season 49 just two weeks prior, hoping transparency would build trust. On Day 4 she beat Colby in a backwards Jenga/stacking challenge and earned a Block-a-Vote, which she kept completely secret. But her tribemates (especially Rick Devens) found her story about the Journey unconvincing and suspect she's hiding something.

## Episode 2

Savannah correctly identified that Cirie was protecting Ozzy, making her a target for speaking the truth. Cirie orchestrated a vote flip to eliminate Savannah instead of Ozzy or Joe. Savannah did not play her Block-a-Vote or Shot in the Dark -- she later said the Block-a-Vote "wouldn't have made a difference" since the vote was going to be lopsided either way, and she "literally forgot" the Shot in the Dark existed. She was voted out 5-1 with her lone vote going to Ozzy.
