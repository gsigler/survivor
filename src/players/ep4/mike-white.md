## Episode 1

Mike employed a deliberate low-profile "goofball" strategy. His new six-pack physique surprised everyone (Charlie: "I guess he's ripped now?"). He bonded with Dee over their shared big-target vulnerability and approached Charlie about working together. His boldest move: revealing to others that Coach embellished his supply challenge victory, positioning himself as the truth-teller while undermining Coach's credibility.

## Episode 2

Mike continued to stoke the flames between Coach and Ozzy during the reward challenge, enjoying the drama while staying safely in the middle. His role as an instigator and information broker keeps him entertained and well-positioned. Kalo won both challenges, avoiding any need to show his hand.

## Episode 3

The swap placed Mike on new Vatu and reunited him with his David vs. Goliath castmates Christian and Angelina -- though as Christian noted, Mike and Angelina had both tried to vote him out multiple times in Season 37. Mike emerged as the strategic mastermind of the vote. He told Q, Ozzy, and Stephenie he would vote for Stephenie as a smokescreen, while secretly working with Christian, Angelina, and Emily to target Q. When Q actually told Mike to vote for him (part of Q's convoluted tie-forcing plan), it only confirmed Mike's read. The plan worked perfectly: Q was voted out 5-1.
