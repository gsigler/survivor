## Episode 1

Rizo started on the outs -- Colby called him "annoying as hell" for talking too much and doing too little work. But by Day 2 the dynamic shifted. Rizo held a "tribe fashion show to boost morale," and Colby began to see him as "a younger version of himself" (Rizo is 25, the same age Colby was on Season 2). Colby took him under his wing, and Rizo was emotional about the bond, saying it "felt like talking to his dad."

## Episode 2

Rizo was formally recruited into the Vatu majority alliance (Q, Colby, Genevieve, Stephenie). He has been "course-correcting" his aggressive behavior from Season 49, taking a more measured approach. His bond with Colby continues to anchor him in the game. Vatu won immunity again.

## Episode 3

The swap isolated Rizo on new Cila as the only Vatu member, alongside Cirie and Rick (Cila) and four Kalo players (Charlie, Dee, Jonathan, Kamilla). In a nighttime conversation, Rizo revealed he had voted against his Season 49 bestie Sophie at Final Tribal Council. He then asked Charlie about his relationship with Maria from Season 46 -- an unwitting trigger for Charlie's deepest Survivor trauma about being screwed by a close ally at FTC. Charlie's trust in Rizo immediately evaporated, making Rizo the likely first boot if Cila goes to Tribal. During the immunity challenge, Rizo took a ring to the head during the blindfolded portion. Cila won immunity, buying him time.
