## Episode 1

Kyle was a central figure building the majority alliance, recruiting Colby, Stephenie, Genevieve, and Q. He viewed Genevieve as his top strategic partner ("his Kamilla of Survivor 50"). During the immunity challenge he jumped off a muddy wall to help a struggling tribemate and ruptured his Achilles tendon. An ultrasound on Day 2 confirmed a full tear. Despite his reluctance to leave, he was pulled from the game -- the second departure of the season.
