## Episode 1

Q stepped up for the supply challenge and was sent to Exile with Ozzy. He agreed to trade his vote for camp supplies while Ozzy took the Extra Vote. When he returned to camp, he initially lied about what happened, fabricating a story about a coconut-carrying challenge. After Kyle brought him into the majority alliance, Q came clean about losing his vote. When Colby then openly told the tribe about his own lost vote, Q was alarmed, realizing the majority was severely vote-deficient.

## Episode 2

Q remained part of the Vatu majority as the core four (Q, Colby, Genevieve, Stephenie) recruited Rizo into their alliance. Vatu won immunity again, so Q's lost vote was not tested. He is "course-correcting" his aggressive behavior from Season 46, playing a more measured game.
