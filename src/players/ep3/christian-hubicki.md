## Episode 1

Christian made fire using his eyeglasses on Day 1, earning instant goodwill. He was the first to identify Cirie as a threat but stayed quiet, instead using Rick Devens to relay info to Cirie that Jenna was targeting her. This effectively framed Jenna as the sole instigator and led to a unanimous 7-1 vote against her. Jenna believed Christian was her ally and later said he "set her up."

## Episode 2

Christian found Cila's Boomerang Idol and gifted it to Aubry Bracco on Vatu as a "token of trust," hoping to build a cross-tribal bond between fellow superfan strategists. He then hatched a scheme with Rick Devens to plant the idol's parchment at Tribal Council as a decoy for future use. He experienced gastrointestinal distress early in the episode but recovered to play a central strategic role. The vote landed on Savannah as Cirie's campaign succeeded.
