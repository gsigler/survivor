## Episode 1

Charlie had a quieter premiere but bonded closely with Coach, who gave him a toe ring that hadn't been off his body in 37 years. Mike White also approached him about working together. Pre-game intel suggests Jonathan wants to target Charlie early. Kalo won immunity so no Tribal Council.

## Episode 2

Charlie continued to lay low as Kalo won both the reward and immunity challenges, avoiding Tribal Council for the second straight week. A generational divide is emerging on the tribe, with the younger players (Charlie, Dee, Tiffany, Kamilla) on one side and the older players (Coach, Chrissy) on the other. Charlie's position between the two groups makes him a potential swing vote.
