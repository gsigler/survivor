## Episode 1

Rick was the key information broker of the premiere. After Christian told him Jenna was targeting Cirie, Rick went directly to Cirie and tipped her off, sealing Jenna's fate. He also noticed that Savannah's story about her advantage challenge didn't add up, signaling she's hiding something. He and Emily discussed targeting Ozzy as a long-term threat.

## Episode 2

Rick's "nerd alliance" with Christian deepened. After Christian found the Boomerang Idol, they devised a plan to plant the idol parchment at Tribal Council as a decoy for later use. Rick also feuded with Joe Hunter over whispered side conversations with Savannah, creating a rift between them. He voted with the majority to eliminate Savannah.
