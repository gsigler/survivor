## Episode 1

Chrissy was the most vocal early strategist on Kalo, immediately pushing to target Dee as the tribe's first boot due to her winner status. She aligned with Coach as her "number one draft pick" and connected with Jonathan about getting Dee out. However, the New Era women's alliance (Dee, Tiffany, Kamilla) identified Chrissy as THEIR primary target, remembering how she gunned for younger women in HHH.

## Episode 2

Chrissy's overbearing "mothering" style alienated tribemates. Dee labeled her "a little too much" for constantly taking care of everyone. The generational divide on Kalo put Chrissy firmly on the older side alongside Coach, with the younger players growing wary. Kalo won both challenges, keeping her safe for now.
