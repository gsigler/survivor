About

Dee Valladares is the Sole Survivor of Season 45 and one of three winners competing on Season 50. The Miami entrepreneur played a dominant strategic game in her first outing, earning the title with a decisive jury vote. As a winner, she enters the game with a massive target on her back.

Season 50 Game

Jonathan Young immediately identified Dee as the biggest threat on Kalo and pitched her name to Mike White and Coach. Dee found early allies in Tiffany Ervin and Kamilla Karthigesu, who were interested in joining her in a cutthroat strategic approach.
