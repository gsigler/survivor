About

Genevieve Mushaluk was one of the villains of Season 47, the corporate lawyer placing 5th and becoming a jury member. She returns almost immediately after her debut season, and her sharp strategic instincts make her a player to watch.

Season 50 Game

Genevieve made an early splash by finding the Billie Eilish Boomerang Idol while walking along the beach. The idol's rules required her to gift it to a player on another tribe — she strategically chose Ozzy Lusth, knowing he was once voted out with an idol in his pocket. If Ozzy is voted out with the idol, it returns to Genevieve. She also formed a tight alliance with Kyle Fraser, Colby, Stephenie, and Q on the Vatu tribe.
