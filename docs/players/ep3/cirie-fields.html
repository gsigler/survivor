Episode 1

Cirie was Jenna's primary target from Day 1. She struggled in the immunity challenge (specifically the monkey's fist obstacle), directly causing Cila to lose and go to Tribal. She was saved when Rick tipped her off about Jenna's plan, and the tribe flipped to vote Jenna out instead. Despite surviving, everyone knows she's a challenge liability.

Episode 2

Cirie showed why she's one of the best to ever play. When the tribe debated targeting Joe, Savannah, or Ozzy, Cirie went to work protecting her ride-or-die. In just a few conversations she redirected the entire vote onto Savannah. Ozzy rewarded her loyalty by giving her his Extra Vote — her first advantage in five seasons of Survivor. The vote landed 5-1 on Savannah with one stray vote on Ozzy.

Advantages

  • Extra Vote — Gifted by Ozzy Lusth. Can cast an additional vote at any Tribal Council.

Alliances

Ride-or-die with Ozzy Lusth. Now armed with an Extra Vote and having proven she can control a vote, Cirie is in her strongest position yet. The Ozzy-Cirie pair remains a top target for others, but their combined advantages give them real protection.

About

Cirie Fields is one of the most beloved and respected players in Survivor history. She went from a self-described "couch potato" afraid of leaves in Season 12 (Panama) to one of the greatest strategic minds the game has ever seen. Across four previous seasons, she finished 4th (Panama), 3rd (Micronesia), and 6th (Game Changers), never quite reaching the final Tribal Council despite being a masterful player.

Cirie also won The Traitors, but she doesn't consider herself a true "legend" until she has a Survivor win. Season 50 marks her record-tying fifth time playing.
