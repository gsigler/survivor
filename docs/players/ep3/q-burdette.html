Episode 1

Q stepped up for the supply challenge and was sent to Exile with Ozzy. He agreed to trade his vote for camp supplies while Ozzy took the Extra Vote. When he returned to camp, he initially lied about what happened, fabricating a story about a coconut-carrying challenge. After Kyle brought him into the majority alliance, Q came clean about losing his vote. When Colby then openly told the tribe about his own lost vote, Q was alarmed, realizing the majority was severely vote-deficient.

Episode 2

Q remained part of the Vatu majority as the core four (Q, Colby, Genevieve, Stephenie) recruited Rizo into their alliance. Vatu won immunity again, so Q's lost vote was not tested. He is "course-correcting" his aggressive behavior from Season 46, playing a more measured game.

Disadvantages

  • Lost Vote — Traded his vote for camp supplies on Exile Island. Cannot vote at his next Tribal Council.

Alliances

Part of the Vatu majority with Colby, Stephenie, Genevieve, and Rizo. Still voteless at his next Tribal. A swap could put him on a tribe with former rival Tiffany Ervin or separate him from his allies, making his lost vote even more dangerous.

About

Q Burdette brought plenty of charm and controversy to Season 46, finishing 6th. He's perhaps best remembered for being the subject of another player's ire when he won an Applebee's reward. His strategy for Season 50 is clear: "If you've ever sat at the end, whether you've won, lost or whatever … I don't want to work with you long term. I want you out of the game." His former rival Tiffany Ervin is on the opposing Kalo tribe.
