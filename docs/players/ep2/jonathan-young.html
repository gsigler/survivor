Episode 1

Jonathan played a measured game, letting others come to him. He connected with Coach and Chrissy separately to build a voting bloc against Dee. Coach promised he'd "never vote him out." In the immunity challenge, Jonathan served as a "human ladder" to help Kalo scale the wall, and his physical dominance helped carry them to first place. Pre-game intel also suggests he wants to target Charlie.

Alliances

Bridge player in the anti-Dee coalition with Coach and Chrissy. Very strong position — physical asset who keeps the tribe winning, multiple alliance connections, and not anyone's named target.

About

Jonathan Young burst onto the Survivor scene in Season 42 as one of the most physically dominant players in recent memory. He made it to Day 25 before being eliminated after losing the fire-making challenge. Jeff Probst has called him one of the most "dominant" players the show has seen. Before Season 50, Boston Rob wrote Jonathan a letter full of strategic advice to read on the plane.
