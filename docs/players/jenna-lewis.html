About

Jenna Lewis-Dougherty is a Survivor original, having competed on the very first season (Borneo) back in 2000. She returned for All-Stars (Season 8) and came into Season 50 with reverence from the cast as a representative of the show's earliest days.

Season 50 Game

Jenna came in aggressively, immediately targeting Cirie Fields and throwing her name around camp from Day 1. This aggressive approach backfired — the rest of the Cila tribe turned on her, and she was voted out in a decisive 7-1 vote at the season's first Tribal Council. In her exit interview, Jenna said she hopes Cila loses every remaining challenge.
