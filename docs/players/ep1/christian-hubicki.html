About

Christian Hubicki became a fan favorite on David vs. Goliath with his endearing personality and sharp strategic mind. The robotics professor finished 7th in his first outing and has been one of the most-requested returnees since. He learned from castmate Mike White the concept of "narrative warfare" and hopes to deploy it this time around.

Christian is keeping one major secret: his six-week-old baby at home.
