About

Ozzy Lusth is one of the most physically dominant players in Survivor history. His challenge prowess is legendary — from climbing trees to catch fish to dominating immunity runs. He finished as runner-up on Cook Islands and 4th on South Pacific but has never won the title of Sole Survivor. Now the owner of a music venue, tiki bar, and restaurant called Xolazul, Ozzy returns for a record-tying fifth time.

Season 50 Game

Ozzy volunteered for the supply challenge and dominated, securing all three bags before the other tribes could finish. He ended up with an Extra Vote advantage from an Exile boat twist. He also received the Billie Eilish Boomerang Idol, gifted to him by Genevieve Mushaluk (who found it on the Vatu beach). The idol cannot be given away and must be played on himself — fitting, given Ozzy was once voted out with an idol in his pocket.
