Episode 1 Eliminations

Voted Out: Jenna Lewis-Dougherty

The sole Season 1 representative came in too aggressively, immediately targeting Cirie Fields. The rest of the Cila tribe turned on Jenna instead, sending her home in a decisive 7-1 vote. In her exit interview, Jenna said she hopes Cila loses every challenge going forward.

Medical Evacuation: Kyle Fraser

Season 48 winner Kyle Fraser ruptured his Achilles tendon during the first immunity challenge. Medical tests confirmed the severity of the injury, requiring surgery and roughly three months of recovery. Kyle was evacuated from the game on Day 4.
